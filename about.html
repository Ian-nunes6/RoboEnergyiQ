About RoboEnergiQ

RoboEnergiQ is a personal robotics and automation project developed by Ian Nunes for the WSRO Nationals ‚Äî Young Scientist Algorithm Open category.

The project focuses on energy optimization in automated packaging and sorting systems. RoboEnergiQ uses a QR-based robotic arm, real-time power monitoring (INA219), and camera-based QR recognition to identify, classify, and sort items while minimizing energy consumption.

Designed and built entirely by me, RoboEnergiQ demonstrates how intelligent algorithms, efficient hardware choices, and careful energy profiling can produce more sustainable robotics.
